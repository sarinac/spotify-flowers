Music Visualization with Spotify

In The Heights

by Anthony Ramos

This arrangement consists of 18 flowers, each representing a section in the music with its stem positioned in order of when it plays. The size of the flower corresponds to the loudness of the section.

Each of the 325 petals is a musical bar, which progresses clockwise within each flower. The colors in each petal represent different pitches moving from the center to the tips.
